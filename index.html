About RESTimeReminder

RESTimeReminder is a single-day focus timer designed to help users maintain productivity and encourage regular breaks.
Timer records reset every 24 hours, so you don't need to worry about yesterday's performance. Today is the only timeline that matters for your focus and your health.
Informed by research in student focus, fatigue, and motivation, this timer automatically sets a break session after each work session to promote healthy work habits.
RESTimeReminder is a wellbeing and productivity tool created and developed by
Dr Erica Ho, as part of the RESThesis project for supporting research student wellbeing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RESTimeReminder free to use?
Yes, RESTimeReminder is completely free.

Does RESTimeReminder require installation?
No, RESTimeReminder can be used directly within a web browser on computers and mobile devices.

Will my progress sync across devices?
No, your progress is saved locally and not shared across devices. If you clear your browser data or use incognito/private browsing mode, your progress will be lost.

How can I track my progress over weeks, months, or years instead of daily?
You may use RESTimeKeeper which is designed for long-term time tracking.
